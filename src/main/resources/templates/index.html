About

Welcome to the First Annual New Codington Festival! The New Codington Festival is a celebration of the union between the two cities of Southtown and Northtown, guaranteed fun to be had over the New Codington River (formerly the river coder)! The festival is the brainchild of New Codington chamber of commerce and numerous other sponsors all dedicated to the future prosperity of New Codington and the happiness of its citizens.

Along with reserving you ticket for this historic event, you are encouraged (but not obligated) to provide a small donation to help provide for the city's new transition, funds raised will go to different local projects like stimulating local businesses and cleaning up our beloved river. So please give from your heart and enjoy the Festival!

Events

To celebrate the unity of North Town and South Town, the New Codington Festival is proud to offer the following events:

Elephant Rides*: We are excited to say that Garbozo, North Town Zoo's famous elephant, is no longer just eye-candy. Elephant rides will be offered all-day for children and adults.

*Note: Please do not wear any elephant apparel if you are planning to take a ride on Garbozo. Garbozo is wildy territorial (there is a reason why he is kept alone). On a related note, the Codington Chamber of Commerce is not responsible for any injuries sustained while riding Garbozo.

Dunking Booth: Not a fan of our current Mayor, James Hiccup? Now is your chance to let loose on your aggressions. James is both aquaphobic and bathophobic, so any bullseyes are sure to be fun for the whole family.

Face-Painting: There will be a several face painting booths run by renowned South Town tattoo artist Dingus McJones. Whether you are looking for a fun temporary lizard face for the kids or a permanent lizard face solution for adults, we have got you covered.

Carnival Games: The New Codington Festival will be loaded with carnival games that are fun for the whole family! As part of the Codington River Revival Program, all carnival prizes are made from recycled trash from our famous Codington river.

Carnival Style Rides: Looking for a little excitement? Come take a spin on any one of our faboulous carnival rides. Don't worry, they are supposed to make those noises.
